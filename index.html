What we do

VetVision is a veterinary diagnostic tool that can automatically identify broken or fractured bones in animals to speed up the diagnosis process.

What you get

Our tool will reveal the presence, location, and extent of a fracture. This can be used to quickly and effectively determine the type of fracture, whether it's a simple or compound fracture, and to plan appropriate treatment. Additionally, we can help identify any potential complications, such as displaced bone fragments or damage to surrounding tissues.

User stories

As a pet owner I want access to a website where I can monitor the diagnosis of my pets so that I can keep up to date.

As a veterinarian, I want to see the most accurate diagnosis, so that I can give the pets the most effective treatment.
